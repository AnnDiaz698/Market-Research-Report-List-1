<p><p>The safety pre-filled syringe system market is primarily divided into two categories: glass-based and plastic-based syringes. Glass-based syringes are known for their durability, inertness, and ability to protect sensitive medications from leaching. However, they are heavier and can break easily. In contrast, plastic-based syringes are lighter, more flexible, and less prone to breakage, making them suitable for various applications. Both types prioritize safety by minimizing the risk of needle-stick injuries and ensuring medication integrity during storage and transport.</p></p>
