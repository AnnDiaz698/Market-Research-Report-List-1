<p><strong>The Ski Touring Bindings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Bindings</li><li>Tech Bindings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ski touring bindings market is primarily categorized into frame bindings and tech bindings. Frame bindings, often resembling alpine bindings, offer compatibility with standard ski boots and provide stability, making them suitable for those transitioning to backcountry skiing. Conversely, tech bindings are lightweight and designed for specialized touring boots with pin inserts, offering enhanced efficiency and better uphill performance. Skiers choose between these types based on their priorities, such as comfort, weight, and ability to navigate varied terrain.</p></p>
