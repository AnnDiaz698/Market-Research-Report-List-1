<p><p>The luxury auto leasing market comprises three main types: short-term rental, long-term rental, and finance leasing. Short-term rentals provide vehicles for a brief period, typically targeting tourists or those needing a temporary solution. Long-term rentals extend beyond a few months, appealing to individuals and businesses wanting flexibility without commitment. Finance leasing involves a contractual agreement where the lessee pays for the use of the vehicle over a specified term, often leading to ownership at the end, catering to those seeking long-lasting luxury without upfront costs.</p></p>
