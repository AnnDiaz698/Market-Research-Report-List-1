<p><p>Railway Revenue Management Systems (RMS) can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions require installation and maintenance on the railway's local servers, offering greater control and security but necessitating higher initial investment and ongoing IT management. Conversely, cloud-based RMS solutions operate over the internet, allowing for easier scalability, lower upfront costs, and reduced IT overhead. This flexibility facilitates rapid deployment and updates, making them increasingly popular among railway operators seeking efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
