<p><p>The 3D ICs market comprises primarily two types: 3D SiCs (Silicon Chips) and Monolithic 3D ICs. </p><p>3D SiCs leverage stacking different silicon wafers to enhance performance, reduce signal delay, and optimize space, appealing to applications in high-performance computing and mobile devices. In contrast, Monolithic 3D ICs involve fabricating multiple layers of circuitry on a single chip, which allows for lower manufacturing costs and higher integration. Both types aim to improve efficiency, power consumption, and overall device functionality in advanced electronic systems.</p></p>
