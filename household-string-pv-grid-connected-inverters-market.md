<p><p>The household string PV grid-connected inverters market consists of two main types: low-frequency and high-frequency inverters. Low-frequency inverters typically utilize transformers, offering durability and stability in power handling, making them ideal for larger systems. In contrast, high-frequency inverters are transformerless, allowing for smaller, lighter designs with higher efficiency and faster response times. Both types serve to convert solar energy into grid-compatible electricity, catering to different needs in residential solar power installations while enhancing energy management and consumption efficiency.</p></p>
