<p><p>The bariatric surgical devices market encompasses various procedures aimed at treating obesity. Sleeve gastrectomy involves removing a portion of the stomach to limit food intake, promoting weight loss. Gastric bypass creates a small stomach pouch and reroutes intestines, leading to reduced calorie absorption. Gastric banding places an adjustable band around the stomach to control food consumption. Other surgeries may include biliopancreatic diversion and duodenal switch, offering alternative options for weight management and improved metabolic health.</p></p>
