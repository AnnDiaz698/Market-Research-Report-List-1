<p><p>The milk tofu market consists of two primary types: fresh milk tofu and semi-dried milk tofu. Fresh milk tofu is crafted from fresh milk, offering a creamy texture and high moisture content, making it ideal for soups and salads. In contrast, semi-dried milk tofu has a firmer texture due to partial dehydration, enhancing its shelf life and flavor concentration. Both types cater to different culinary applications and consumer preferences, with fresh milk tofu prioritizing taste and texture, while semi-dried emphasizes versatility and convenience.</p></p>
