<p><strong>The Visual Electrophysiology Testing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multifocal Electroretinogram</li><li>Visual-Evoked Responses</li><li>Electroretinogram</li><li>Electro-Oculogram</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Visual electrophysiology testing devices encompass various types that enable the assessment of retinal and visual pathway functionality. The Multifocal Electroretinogram evaluates multiple retinal locations simultaneously, providing detailed maps of retinal health. Visual-Evoked Responses measure electrical activity in the brain's visual cortex in response to visual stimuli, assessing visual pathway integrity. The Electroretinogram records the electrical responses of retinal cells to light, essential for diagnosing retinal diseases. The Electro-Oculogram tracks eye position and movement, aiding in understanding ocular function.</p></p>
