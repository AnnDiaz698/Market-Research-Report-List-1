<p><p>Loan origination software (LOS) is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based LOS allows users to access the software via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced maintenance costs. This model benefits organizations with remote teams or dynamic needs. In contrast, on-premise LOS is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and compliance but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different operational preferences and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
