<p><p>Bulletproof glass, commonly used for security applications, is categorized into two main types: unidirectional and two-way. Unidirectional bulletproof glass is designed to withstand impacts from one side, typically utilized in environments like police vehicles or banks, ensuring protection against external threats while maintaining visibility. In contrast, two-way bulletproof glass offers protection against impacts from both sides, making it suitable for secure facilities where potential threats can come from any direction. Both types provide varying levels of security based on specific needs.</p></p>
