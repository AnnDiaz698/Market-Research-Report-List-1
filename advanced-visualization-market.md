<p><p>The advanced visualization market encompasses various imaging technologies used for diagnostics in healthcare. Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed cross-sectional images, while Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers high-resolution images of soft tissues. X-Ray and Fluoroscopy focus on real-time imaging of internal structures. Ultrasound uses sound waves for imaging soft tissues. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) visualize metabolic activity and blood flow. Lastly, endoscopy allows direct visualization of internal organs, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
