<p><p>The port access needle market comprises three primary types: winged port access needles, angled port access needles, and straight port access needles. Winged needles feature small "wings" for better grip and stability during insertion. Angled needles are designed for specific access angles, facilitating optimal fluid administration. Straight needles provide a direct approach for access into vascular ports. Each type caters to different clinical needs, ensuring effective and efficient access to vascular systems for medication delivery and other therapeutic purposes.</p></p>
