<p><p>The OLED microdisplay market is primarily divided into two types: reflective and transmissive microdisplays. Reflective microdisplays utilize ambient light to display images, enhancing brightness and contrast, making them suitable for various portable applications. In contrast, transmissive microdisplays function by allowing light to pass through the display, offering vibrant colors and high resolution, making them ideal for applications like augmented reality and virtual reality. Both types have distinct advantages tailored to specific market needs and display requirements.</p></p>
