<p><p>The Variable Frequency Servo Drive (VFSD) market is categorized into low voltage and medium voltage segments. Low voltage VFSDs typically operate under 1,000 volts and are widely used in applications like HVAC, conveyors, and pumps due to their cost-effectiveness and easy installation. Medium voltage VFSDs, operating between 1,000 and 10,000 volts, are designed for larger industrial applications, such as motor control in manufacturing and mining.
