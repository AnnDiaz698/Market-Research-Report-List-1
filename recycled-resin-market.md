<p><p>The recycled resin market consists of various types, primarily PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). PET is widely recycled for beverage bottles and food containers, while PP is used in automotive parts and packaging. HDPE is known for its strength and is commonly found in containers and pipes. LDPE, being flexible and durable, is often used in grocery bags and films. Each type plays a crucial role in promoting sustainability and reducing plastic waste.</p></p>
