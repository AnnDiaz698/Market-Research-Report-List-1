<p><p>The soluble meal fibers market includes various types that serve distinct functional and health benefits. Insulin fiber, sourced from chicory roots, aids in blood sugar regulation and promotes gut health. Polydextrose, a synthetic polymer, enhances fiber content in foods while providing low-calorie options. Pectin, found naturally in fruits, is utilized for its gelling properties and health benefits, including cholesterol reduction. Other soluble fibers may include oat beta-glucans and guar gum, offering additional nutritional advantages and diverse applications in food products.</p></p>
