<p><p>In horse racing, various betting markets cater to different strategies. A Win Bet focuses on a horse finishing first. An Each Way Bet covers both first and second place. A Single Bet is a straightforward wager on one outcome. Multiple Bets allow wagering on several outcomes, increasing potential returns. A Straight Forecast requires predicting the exact first and second place finishers, while a Reverse Forecast allows placing bets on either order. A Tricast entails selecting the top three finishers in the correct order.</p></p>
