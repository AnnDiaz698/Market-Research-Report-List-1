The market can be divided into two main types: solid and liquid. Solid MDI is characterized by its higher molecular weight and is often used in rigid foam applications and coatings, while liquid MDI, being more versatile, is commonly employed in flexible foam and adhesives. Both forms serve critical roles in various industries, including automotive, construction, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>
