<p><p>Industrial tilt sensors are crucial for monitoring the orientation of equipment and structures. The market is primarily segmented into single-axis and dual-axis types. Single-axis tilt sensors measure inclination along one direction, making them suitable for applications like slope detection. Dual-axis tilt sensors, on the other hand, measure tilt in two directions, offering more comprehensive data for complex machinery and infrastructure stability. These sensors enhance safety and operational efficiency in various industrial settings, including construction, mining, and automation.</p></p>
