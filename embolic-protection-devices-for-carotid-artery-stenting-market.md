<p><p>Embolic Protection Devices (EPDs) for carotid artery stenting are essential tools that minimize the risk of debris entering the bloodstream during the procedure. Distal occlusion devices capture and retain embolic material downstream at the site of the stenting. In contrast, proximal occlusion devices seal the artery upstream, preventing any debris from migrating through the stent. Together, these devices enhance patient safety by reducing the likelihood of ischemic complications and improving outcomes in carotid artery interventions.</p></p>
