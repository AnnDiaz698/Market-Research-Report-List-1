<p><p>Solar backsheet materials are crucial for photovoltaic panels, providing insulation and protection. The KPK structure combines polyester (PET) with polyvinyl fluoride (PVF) layers, offering durability. KPE structures utilize polyethylene (PE) and enhance moisture resistance. TPE structures feature thermoplastic elastomers, providing flexibility and longevity. PPE structures, made from polyphenylene, deliver high thermal stability. Other types may include various composite materials or specialty coatings tailored for specific environmental conditions, ensuring solar panels perform effectively across diverse applications.</p></p>
