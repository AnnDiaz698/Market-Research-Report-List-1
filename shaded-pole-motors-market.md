<p><p>Shaded pole motors are a type of single-phase AC motor known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. The market is bifurcated into salient pole cover motors and hidden pole cover motors. Salient pole cover motors feature visible poles that project outwards, enhancing cooling and efficiency. In contrast, hidden pole cover motors have poles concealed within the motor's body, providing a more compact design. Both types are widely used in low-power applications, such as fans, small appliances, and electric toys, due to their straightforward operation and reliability.</p></p>
