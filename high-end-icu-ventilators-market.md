<p><ul><li>Invasive Ventilation</li><li>Non-invasive Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end ICU ventilator market is primarily divided into two types: invasive and non-invasive ventilation. Invasive ventilation involves inserting a tube into the patient's airway, providing respiratory support for critically ill patients who cannot breathe adequately on their own. Non-invasive ventilation utilizes masks or nasal interfaces, offering support without intubation, making it suitable for patients with less severe respiratory distress. Both types are essential for managing respiratory failure and optimizing patient outcomes in intensive care settings.</p></p>
