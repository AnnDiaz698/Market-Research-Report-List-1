<p><p>Hospital Real-time Location Systems (RTLS) utilize various technologies to track assets, personnel, and patients within healthcare facilities. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) employs tags and readers for automated identification. Wi-Fi-based systems leverage existing wireless networks for precise positioning. Ultrasound uses sound waves for real-time tracking, while infrared relies on light signals for location identification. Other technologies may include GPS or Bluetooth. Each type offers unique advantages in accuracy, range, and integration, enhancing operational efficiency and improving patient care within hospitals.</p></p>
