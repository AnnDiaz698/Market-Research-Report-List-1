<p><p>The Single Use Laparoscopic Retriever market is divided into two main types: detachable and non-detachable. Detachable retrievers allow surgeons to secure and release the tissue or organ without removing the entire instrument, enhancing efficiency during procedures. In contrast, non-detachable retrievers are designed for single-task use, where the item is removed along with the tissue after retrieval. Both types aim to improve surgical outcomes, reduce infection risks, and streamline operating room processes, catering to various procedural needs.</p></p>
