<p><ul><li>Grinding Wheel Dicing Machine</li><li>Laser Dicing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor dicing equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: grinding wheel dicing machines and laser dicing machines. Grinding wheel dicing machines utilize rotating abrasive wheels to slice semiconductor wafers into individual chips, offering high precision and efficiency, especially for hard materials. In contrast, laser dicing machines use focused laser beams to cut through the wafers, providing flexibility and reducing mechanical stress, making them ideal for intricate designs and sensitive materials. Both technologies are essential for semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
